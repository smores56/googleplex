FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions



This website is like a search engine for bestseller lists, and the books and authors on the lists.
  • Upload bestseller lists to our database
  • Search for bestseller lists, authors and books.
  • Filter lists and books by tags such as genres and dates.
  • Filter authors by ethnicity, gender etc.
  • Download these lists of books, authors and bestsellers to do further data analysis
You do not need an account to search for lists and download them but you will need an account to submit a list.
In the top right corner of the page, there is a Log In button. After clicking, you will see an oprtion to register if you do not already have an account.
In the search bar either at the top of the page or on the center of the main page, type the name of a list, book or author. You will be able to specify which of those three things you are searching for by making a selection using the drop down menu beside the search bar.
First, you will need to register for an account. Then in the top right corner of the webpage, click on Submit a List. Then you will need to select what method you'd like to upload a list as: File, Image or Manually. Follow the rest of the instructions provided on that page.
You will have to contact us to plead your case.
If you need to contact us about a banned account, or if you have any other questions not answered in the FAQ please feel free to email us at bestsellerarchive@gmail.com.
