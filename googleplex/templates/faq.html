FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What is the purpose of this site?

    2. This website is like a search engine for bestseller lists, and the books and authors on the lists.

  2. What can I do on this site?

    3. You can:
    • Upload bestseller lists to our database
    • Search for bestseller lists, authors and books.
    • Filter lists and books by tags such as genres and dates.
    • Filter authors by ethnicity, gender etc.
    • Download these lists of books, authors and bestsellers to do further data analysis

  3. Do I need an account to use this site?

    4. You do not need an account to search for lists and download them but you will need an account to submit a list.

  4. How do I register for an account?

    5. In the top right corner of the page, there is a Log In button. After clicking, you will see an oprtion to register if you do not already have an account.

  5. How do I search for a list/author/bestseller?

    6. In the search bar either at the top of the page or on the center of the main page, type the name of a list, book or author. You will be able to specify which of those three things you are searching for by making a selection using the drop down menu beside the search bar.

  6. How do I add a list/author/bestseller?

    7. First, you will need to register for an account. Then in the top right corner of the webpage, click on Submit a List. Then you will need to select what method you'd like to upload a list as: File, Image or Manually. Follow the rest of the instructions provided on that page.

  7. I've been banned, what do I do?

    8. You will have to email us to plead your case. You can email us at bestsellerarchive@gmail.com.

If you have any questions not answered in the FAQ please feel free to email us at bestsellerarchive@gmail.com.
